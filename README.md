
# AdventureWorks Report - Power BI

## Outdoor Sales Dashboard Report

This Power BI report demonstrates capabilities in **data cleaning**, creating **interactive dashboards**, and using **filters and slicers** to provide dynamic insights. The dashboard is divided into **two main parts**, each offering valuable perspectives on outdoor product sales and performance.

---

## Dashboard Overview

### Part 1: Sales and Product Analysis
<img width="1735" height="981" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ed6e88aa-a9bf-46ae-b383-758223a09b7d" />

1. **Monthly Sales & Profit**  
   - Displays **sales and profit trends** from **July 2019 to May 2020**.  
   - Provides a clear view of **monthly performance** and **profit margins**.

2. **Sales by Product & Category by Country**  
   - Breaks down sales by **product** and **category** across countries.  
   - Example: **US** achieved **$3M** in total yearly sales across all three categories.

3. **Quantity by Category**  
   - Shows the **total quantity sold** by category:  
     - Clothing  
     - Bikes  
     - Accessories  
     - Components

> **Interactive Filters:** Users can select **year** and **country** to visualize sales and profit insights dynamically.

---

### Part 2: Targets and Key Metrics

<img width="1527" height="861" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/807db992-3b4e-4c75-8f14-3a3964366423" />

4. **Monthly Sales vs. Targets**  
   - Displays **sales and targets** from **July 2018 to June 2019**.  
   - Key highlights:  
     - **Highest sales** occurred in **August**.  
     - **Highest targets** were set in **November and December**.

5. **Key Metrics & Insights**  
   - Includes **cards** to showcase **key financial metrics**.  
   - Provides quick insights on **overall sales**, **targets**, **variance**, and **variance margin**.

> **Interactive Filter:** Users can filter by **year** to focus on specific periods of io interest.

---

This dashboard highlights **interactive reporting capabilities** in Power BI, combining **high-level summaries** with **detailed analytical views** for informed decision-making.
